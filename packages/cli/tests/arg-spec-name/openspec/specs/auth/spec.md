# auth Specification

## Purpose
This specification describes how customers sign in and keep access to protected product areas.

## Requirements

### Requirement: The system SHALL let a customer sign in with a verified account and reach protected features without unnecessary friction
The system SHALL let a customer sign in with a verified account and reach protected features without unnecessary friction.

#### Scenario: A signed-in customer opens the account area
- **WHEN** a customer signs in with valid credentials
- **THEN** the account area is shown
